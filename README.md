Founded in 2026, our company was created to address the growing challenge of plastic waste by reducing
            landfill pollution and advancing sustainability through recycling. Established by Stephen Roy, Stanley
            Okello, Shadrack Solonka, and Peter Mwangangi, the company is built on a shared vision of environmental
            responsibility and innovation. Through circular economy practices, we transform plastic waste into valuable
            resources, conserve natural materials, create green employment opportunities, and deliver lasting
            environmental and social impact for present and future generations.
