ABOUT US

OUR PURPOSE

Our purpose is to build trust and credibility.

OUR STORY

Founded in 2026, our company was created to address the growing challenge of plastic waste by reducing landfill pollution and advancing sustainability through recycling. Established by Stephen Roy, Stanley Okello, Shadrack Solonka, and Peter Mwangangi, the company is built on a shared vision of environmental responsibility and innovation. Through circular economy practices, we transform plastic waste into valuable resources, conserve natural materials, create green employment opportunities, and deliver lasting environmental and social impact for present and future generations.

OUR MISSION

To create a cleaner, greener future through responsible recycling.

OUR VISION

To become the leading recycling partner in the region.
