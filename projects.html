This project focuses on implementing a comprehensive recycling program in schools to reduce waste and promote environmental awareness.we were able to install recycling bins in 10 schools.Reduced waste by 40%.

Corporate Waste Audit

This project involves conducting a comprehensive audit of corporate waste management practices to identify inefficiencies and opportunities for improvement. We helped companies reduce their waste output by 30% through better sorting and recycling protocols.

Community Clean-Up Drive

This project involved organizing community clean-up drives in local neighborhoods to remove litter and promote environmental stewardship. We successfully cleaned up over 500 acres of public land and engaged more than 200 volunteers.Collected 700 kgs of waste from public parks.
